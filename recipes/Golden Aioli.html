Golden Aioli

Home

Description

Golden aioli is a creamy, tangy sauce with a hint of sweetness that comes together in just minutes. Use as a spread for sandwiches, a dip for fries and vegetables, or serve as a side for grilled meats and seafood.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Stir together 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  2. 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  3. 1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
  4. 1 teaspoon sugar in a small bowl
